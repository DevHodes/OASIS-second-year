THE STORY...

Oasis were a Manchester-based English rock band from 1991-to 2009. The group went through several ‎lineup changes during the course of their career, but the initial core consisted of Noel Gallagher (lead ‎guitar, vocals), Liam Gallagher (vocals, tambourine), Paul Arthurs (guitar), Paul McGuigan (bass guitar), ‎and Tony McCarroll (drums).‎

As Arthurs described it, Oasis generated a sound "so devoid of finesse and complexity that it came out ‎sounding pretty much unstoppable." They released numerous songs spanning eight albums. They ‎defined a generation and continue to impact musicians all over the world - their legacy lives on. ‎

The band had; over 70 million records sold; more than 30 wins from the Brits, MTV, NME, and Q; ‎Britain's best-selling debut album, quickly followed by Britain's best-selling record overall; and Guinness ‎World Records entry for having 22 successive Top Ten hits. ‎
